Key Benefits

Decoupling

Components are shielded from the complexities of the subsystem. You can refactor the underlying services without touching any components, as long as the Facade's public API remains the same.

Simplicity & Readability

Component logic becomes cleaner and more focused on the presentation layer. Business logic is centralized in the Facade, making the codebase easier to understand and reason about.

Improved Testability

When testing a component, you only need to mock a single Facade instead of multiple individual services. This drastically simplifies test setup and maintenance.

Centralized Logic

Provides a single place to manage complex interactions, state, and caching strategies related to a specific domain, preventing logic duplication across multiple components.
