Key Takeaways

The Facade is not just a pattern; it's a strategy for managing complexity and building scalable, maintainable Angular applications.

✓ Use a Facade when:

  • A component starts depending on many services.
  • You want to simplify a complex subsystem.
  • You need to decouple your components from underlying APIs or state management.

✗ Avoid a Facade when:

  • The interaction is already very simple (e.g., calling one method on one service).
  • You need direct, fine-grained control over the underlying services from the component.
